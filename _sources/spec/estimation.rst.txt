Estimation Methods under Unconfoundedness
=========================================

This section contains methods for estimating (heterogeneous) treatment effects,
whose theoretical guarantees are valid only when all potential confounders/controls (factors that simultaneously
had a direct effect on the treatment decision in the collected data and the observed outcome) are observed.
